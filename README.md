# Sales and Revenue Report Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview
This project delivers a **professional business intelligence dashboard** built in **Power BI** to analyze sales, revenue, and profit distribution across channels, regions, cities, and products.  
It is designed for executive use, providing actionable insights at both high-level and granular detail.

---

## 🔑 Key Features
- **Channel Analysis**: Compare revenue and budget allocation across Wholesale, Distributor, and Export.
- **Regional Distribution**: Visualize channel performance across West, South, Midwest, and Northeast.
- **City Segmentation**: Identify top 10 and least 10 revenue-generating cities.
- **Product Profitability**: Highlight top 5 profit-generating products.
- **Profit Trend**: Track monthly profit changes to detect risks and opportunities.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **Power BI** (DAX measures, Top N filters, custom visuals)
- **SQL** (data integration and preprocessing)
- **Python (pandas, seaborn)** for data cleaning and exploratory analysis
- **Excel/WPS** for initial dataset preparation

---

## 📈 Insights
- Wholesale contributes **~53% of profit**, making it the dominant channel.
- Revenue is concentrated in a few states (California, Illinois, Florida, Texas).
- Products **26, 25, 13, 14, 5** generate the majority of profit.
- Profit trend shows a **decline from Jan–Jul**, requiring strategic review.
